# Cluster 1: Anger & the Second Arrow

The root cluster: anger, aversion, patience, and the two-arrows teaching.

## The source teaching

- **The Arrow (Sallatha Sutta, SN 36:6) — trans. Thanissaro Bhikkhu** — https://www.dhammatalks.org/suttas/SN/SN36_6.html
  The original two-arrows text: pain is the first arrow; the sorrow, resistance, and
  distraction we add are the second. Short. Read it once early, return often.
  (Alternate translation by Bhikkhu Sujato: https://suttacentral.net/sn36.6/en/sujato)

## Talks

- **Patience — Thanissaro Bhikkhu (2026-06-12, morning talk)** — https://www.dhammatalks.org/audio/morning/2026/260612-patience.html
  Already in the library. Patience as not wearing the burden all the time.
  Reach for it when practice feels like grim endurance.

- **Anger Issues — Thanissaro Bhikkhu (2019-05-31, morning talk)** — https://www.dhammatalks.org/audio/morning/2019/190531-anger-issues.html
  A short, direct look at working with anger as it arises, rather than justifying it.
  Reach for it when anger feels righteous and you want a second opinion.

- **Killing Anger — Thanissaro Bhikkhu (2016-02-05, morning talk)** — https://www.dhammatalks.org/audio/morning/2016/160205-killing-anger.html
  On the Buddha's teaching that anger is the one thing worth killing — how to end
  it without suppressing it. Reach for it when anger has already flared and you
  need a way to put it down.

- **Patience & Goodwill — Thanissaro Bhikkhu (2026-04-10, morning talk)** — https://www.dhammatalks.org/audio/morning/2026/260410-patience-goodwill.html
  Pairs the two antidotes: patience toward what can't change yet, goodwill toward
  the people involved. Reach for it when someone specific keeps setting you off.

- **Anger Eating Demons — Ajahn Brahm (2011, 56 min)** — https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=me7Wm5LOpx0
  The classic story-rich Brahm talk: the demon that grows on anger and shrinks
  on kindness. Reach for it when you want the teaching carried by a story
  instead of an argument.

- **Anger and Forgiveness — Ajahn Brahm (2023, 64 min)** — https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d4JAjEj2d_c
  A full Friday-night talk connecting letting go of anger to forgiving — others
  and yourself. Reach for it when the anger is old and has hardened into a grudge.

- **Dealing with people that irritate us — Ajahn Brahm (4 min clip)** — https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Haj6wtNSP2k
  A short clip; a good first taste of Brahm's style on this theme.
  Reach for it when you have four minutes and one irritating person on your mind.
